The Gentelmen's Club

The Gentleman’s Club, (TGC for short), a shop where men find what they need to be in their best look. TGC offers everything from Oxford Long Sleeve Shirts to Bracelets and leather goods.

The Challange

TGC was experiencing some issues with their old logo due to a bad design. The challenge was to redesign the logo in a way customers won’t notice the change.

The Process

After some conversations with the founder to understand the current brand and what we’re dealing with, we started the research phase conducting research with people who are the targeted market to understand how they think and why they buy the product. After building the brand we started working on the visual brand and experimented with the logo and we came up with a support element to support the application.
